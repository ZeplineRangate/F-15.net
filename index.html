FAQ

The F-15 Eagle is a twin-engine tactical fighter aircraft designed for air superiority.

The aircraft entered service with the USAF in 1976.

Major variants include F-15A, F-15C, F-15E, and F-15EX.

The F-15 can reach speeds of Mach 2.5.
